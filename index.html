WASHU BIZTECH

BizTech is currently planning networking nights, information sessions, and more Get notify about these events by joining our roster on WUGO.

WashU BizTech, short for Business and Technology Club, was founded in 2020 with a desire to promote the intersection of business and technology. BizTech believes in the future of a world built on the synergy between these two powerful fields.

We welcome students of all backgrounds and pride ourselves on the inclusivity of our network and community. We believe in the importance of sharing resources and opportunities to all those interested in business and technology!
